What Is An Indian Wedding ?

A wedding in India is much more than a celebration—it is the beautiful beginning of a lifelong journey where two families come together to create new relationships, memories, and traditions. Every smile, every ceremony, and every ritual reflects the values and cultural heritage passed down through generations.

India is home to a rich diversity of religions, communities, and cultures, which is why no two weddings are exactly the same. From vibrant pre-wedding celebrations to sacred wedding ceremonies, each ritual has its own story, purpose, and significance. While the customs may vary across different regions and traditions, they all share one common message—love, respect, togetherness, and the blessing of a happy new beginning.

This website takes you on a journey through the beautiful rituals and traditions that make Indian weddings one of the most colourful, meaningful, and unforgettable celebrations in the world.
