Why Indian Weddings Are Special ❤️ ?

💖They celebrate the union of two families, not just two individuals..

🌍 Every region and community has its own unique wedding traditions.

🎨 Indian weddings are known for their vibrant colors, music, and cultural diversity.

📖 Many traditions have been passed down through generations


Short Overview 📋

An Indian wedding is a beautiful celebration of love and commitment. It includes several pre-wedding, wedding, and post-wedding rituals. Each ritual has a special meaning and reflects the rich cultural heritage and values of India.
