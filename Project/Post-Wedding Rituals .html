-- Post-Wedding Rituals 🎉


## Vidaai 👋

Vidaai is one of the most emotional moments of an Indian wedding. The bride bids farewell to her parents and family as she leaves for her new home. Filled with tears, hugs, and heartfelt blessings, this ceremony marks the end of one chapter and the beginning of a new journey in her married life.



## Griha Pravesh 🏡

Griha Pravesh is the traditional welcome of the bride into her new home. She is greeted by her in-laws with aarti and blessings, and in many families, she gently kicks a pot of rice before entering with her right foot. This ritual symbolizes prosperity, happiness, and a warm beginning in her new family.



## Aeki Beki (Ring Finding Game) 💍

The bride and groom search for a ring hidden in a bowl of milk, water, and flower petals. Whoever finds the ring first is playfully teased as the one who will have the upper hand in the marriage.It helps the newly married couple bond and creates joyful memories with both families.



## Untying the Knot 🧵 (Fun Game)

The bride and groom are asked to untie a decorative knot tied in a cloth or ribbon. They work together to complete the task.It symbolizes teamwork, patience, and solving life's challenges together.



## Wedding Reception 🎉

The Wedding Reception is a grand celebration held after the wedding ceremony, where the newly married couple is introduced to relatives, friends, and well-wishers. Guests offer their blessings, enjoy delicious food, music, and celebrations, making it a joyful conclusion to the wedding festivities.
